Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

Step 2: Heat a pan and pour the batter.

Step 3: Cook until golden on both sides.

Creamy Pasta

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 250 g
Cream 200 ml
Cheese 100 g

Step 1: Boil pasta until soft.

Step 2: Heat cream and add cheese.

Step 3: Mix pasta with sauce and serve.
